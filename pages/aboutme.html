    About Me

His extensive background in data structures and algorithms, software design patterns and varying programming paradigms have allowed hiim to turn his creative thoughts into reality.. Simon is driven by his innovative spirit and considers himself a “forever student,” eager to build the foundations of his career and to stay in tune with the constantly changing environment in the world of tech.
